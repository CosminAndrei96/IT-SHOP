    About IT SHOP

    IT SHOP is an online store from BUCHAREST, with deliveries all over the country, founded in the summer of 2008 and recognized in a short time as one of the most important online stores in Romania due to the quality of services offered to customers and the awards obtained in the eCommerce industry.

    Our mission is to provide you with a new brand of online shopping that will be as fun as using the mouse through your favorite malls, comfortably and safely.

    The prizes won by IT SHOP

    Shop of the year 2009

    IT SHOP was named the winner of the 2009 eCommerce Awards Gala and was named the shop of the year 2009. The eCommerce Awards Gala is an annual competition dedicated to the eCommerce industry. In 2009, GPeC was in its 4th edition organized by Andrei Radu - CEO of Link2eCommerce.

    Best Start-up Store 2008

    In the first year of its existence, IT SHOP won the title of the best start-up store at the eCommerce Awards Gala. This award is given to the winner in the category of shops with less than a year of experience.

    Second place for the best design in 2008

    IT SHOP won the 2nd place for the best design, awarded in the Ro Web Design Awards 2008 competition organized by the Realitatea-Catavencu group.
